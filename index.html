01 — The Problem

A broken process in a moment
of global energy emergency

The 2026 Iran shock has made the electric transition no longer deferrable. Brent at $100/barrel, TTF at €72/MWh: EV drivers today are immune. Those dependent on hydrocarbons pay the price of dependency every single day.

// Geopolitical Context — April 2026

The largest energy shock in history accelerates everything

The IEA has described the Iranian crisis as the largest supply disruption in the history of energy markets. Brent has surpassed $100/barrel (+50% since January 2026), TTF has reached €72/MWh. Italy risks €10 billion in additional energy costs in 2026. ChargeBid is national energy security infrastructure, not merely an operational efficiency platform.

// CPO Pain

High costs, fragmented and underserved market

Internal location search teams cost €200–400K/year. Despite this, they fail to cover the long tail of the market: the majority of available locations belong to small owners and small groups, not large retailers. ChargeBid at €10K in year one is structurally cheaper and covers the segment that internal teams cannot reach.

// Landlord Pain

One offer, no benchmark, too much bureaucracy

Property owners receive at most one proposal, with no way to compare terms, assess CPO reliability, or get legal support. After 10–12 months of exclusivity, the option often lapses and the landlord starts from scratch — losing time and passive income opportunities.

// OEM Urgency

BYD and Asian OEMs: the window closes in 6–9 months

Chinese manufacturers have capital and urgency but zero relationships with European landlords. ChargeBid can get BYD up and running with hundreds of locations in letting within 3 months. Without a structured solution, they will build alternative internal processes within 6–9 months — a window that will not reopen.

02b — Platform Sides

A three-sided platform
serving every market actor

Beyond the core landlord–CPO marketplace, ChargeBid activates two additional high-potential value streams that no other platform addresses today.

Side 01 — Landlord

Property owners list their locations

Property owners list their sites and receive competitive offers from multiple CPOs simultaneously. Landlords with existing installations get free access in exchange for feedback on their CPO experience — feeding the platform's proprietary rating from day 1.

  • List location with GPS, photos and specs
  • Receive and compare competitive offers from multiple CPOs
  • Feedback on existing installations = free access
  • Legal and contractual support assisted by the platform
  • Passive income with zero search effort
Side 02 — CPO (Subscription)

Operators subscribe to access pre-validated locations

Subscribed CPOs access all locations with full geo-intelligence analysis. The subscription starts at €10,000 in year one — a low barrier to demonstrate value — with a 100% increase in year two once results are established.

  • Full scoring: traffic, power, land registry, permits, ROI

  • NPS score and reputation analytics sold as premium add-on
  • Y1: €10,000 · Y2: €20,000 · Y3+: €25,000–40,000

  • CPO leases one or more chargers on an active location
  • CPO sells a contracted location no longer in development plan
  • Immediate targets: BYD, NIO, CATL and OEMs with proprietary networks
  • Time to market for OEMs: 3 months vs 18–24 months traditional
  • ChargeBid commission retained on transaction (clearing house model)
02c — Customer Journey & Contrattualistica

How it works
for Landlords and CPOs

Two distinct journeys, protected by anti-abuse mechanisms and standard contracts designed to become the sector benchmark.

JOURNEY — LANDLORD
1
List the location on ChargeBid
Upload address, photos, technical specs and preferences. Accept the listing terms: by accepting, the landlord grants ChargeBid exclusive rights over the location for 12 months. If the location is transferred to a third party before expiry, the landlord is liable for a contractual penalty.
2
Receives offers from subscribed CPOs
The location is visible to CPOs with an active subscription. The landlord receives competitive offers with revenue share, installation timelines, charger type and CPO rating — all in a single dashboard.
3
Chooses and signs digitally
Standard ChargeBid contract generated automatically: includes revenue sharing, sub-lease, letting and contract transfer clauses. The landlord signs with integrated digital signature — no notarial involvement required at the initial stage.
4
Collects revenue share via ChargeBid
The CPO pays the annual revenue share through the platform. ChargeBid retains its commission and credits the net amount to the landlord — automatic, tracked, frictionless.
⚠ ANTI-ABUSE LANDLORD
→ 12-month exclusivity from listing: transferring the location off-platform incurs a penalty
→ Identity and property ownership verification mandatory at listing
→ Maximum N locations per unverified user (anti-spam limit)
→ Already transferred locations cannot be re-listed for 24 months
JOURNEY — CPO
1
Subscribes — or is onboarded for free (Y1)

2
Views locations — with detail level based on subscription
With subscription: exact address, photos, technical data, full geo-intelligence, estimated ROI, landlord NPS.
Without subscription: geographic area only with 3 km approximation — strong incentive to subscribe.
3
Submits offer and negotiates on the platform
The CPO submits their offer with investment model, proposed revenue share, charger type and timelines. All communications with the landlord take place on ChargeBid — no direct off-platform contact before signing.
4
Signs, pays and manages via clearing house
Signs the standard ChargeBid contract. Pays the closing fee and annual revenue share through the platform. ChargeBid retains its commission on both streams automatically.
⚠ ANTI-ABUSE CPO
→ Direct contact with landlord off-platform forbidden before signing: contractual penalty
→ Deal closed off-platform after internal contact: closing fee still owed
→ Rating and NPS visible to all: improper behaviour penalises ranking and visibility
→ Revenue share verified via clearing house: no possibility of under-reporting
CHARGEBID STANDARD CONTRACTS
The standard that will become the sector benchmark

ChargeBid develops standard contracts designed to cover all scenarios in the lifecycle of an EV location. The goal is to become the reference template adopted by CPOs, landlords and property managers across Europe — just as ISDA contracts became the standard in the derivatives market.

CLAUSES COVERED BY THE STANDARD CONTRACT
→ Revenue Sharing: percentage of charging revenues paid to the landlord, with calculation and payment mechanism via ChargeBid clearing house
→ Contract transfer: ability for the CPO to assign the contract to a third party (OEM, other CPO) with landlord notification and platform approval
→ Sub-lease: landlord may sub-lease portions of the location for installation, with clear rules on maintenance and liability
→ Charger letting: CPO may lease one or more installed chargers to third-party operators (OEMs, fleet managers), with automatic commission via ChargeBid
→ Listing exclusivity: 12 months from publication on ChargeBid, with penalty for off-platform transfer

04 — Business Model

Five revenue streams
on two clearing houses

ChargeBid is a clearing house on two distinct payment streams: the one-off closing fee and the recurring annual revenue share. On both, it automatically retains its commission — the Stripe model applied to EV infrastructure.

CPO Subscription

€10K→€20K→€40K

Incremental year-on-year. Y1 low barrier, Y2 +100% anchored to results, Y3+ at full rate.

Deal Closing Fee

€500–1.000 / loc.

One-off at signing. ChargeBid acts as clearing house: retains commission on the CPO-to-landlord fee payment.

Annual Revenue Share

0.3% charging rev.

The CPO pays the landlord an annual % of charging revenues. ChargeBid manages the flow and retains its share — automatic, recurring, no billing integrations needed.

OEM/CPO Letting

% of transaction

Secondary market: CPO leases chargers to OEMs/new entrants. ChargeBid also acts as clearing house on this stream.

NPS Analytics

Premium add-on

Proprietary rating and NPS score sold to CPOs. A dataset impossible to replicate, built from day one thanks to landlord feedback.
